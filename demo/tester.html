Annoto Loader — interactive tester

Self-contained tester for the annoto-loader plugin. It registers the plugin and enables it purely through the player plugins config — there is no host-page Annoto script, mirroring how a uiConf-bundled plugin behaves inside the Kaltura IFrame embed.

The sign-in methods shown (Anonymous, OAuth/social, Email login) are whatever is configured for this clientId in the Annoto dashboard.

Defaults point at Annoto's Kaltura test player. Replace with your own to test your account.
